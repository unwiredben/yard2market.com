Delivery and Pickup Information

Deliveries are on Fridays from 1pm to 3pm ONLY.

ZIP CodesDelivery Charge
78756 / 78757$3 or FREE with $10 minimum purchase
78701 / 78702 / 78703 / 78721 / 78722 / 78723 / 78731 / 78751 / 78752 / 78759$5
Other locationsDelivery not available at this time

Orders can also be picked up at our distribution site in the 78756 zipcode on Fridays from 2pm to 8pm.

In order to ensure the freshness and safety of your harvest, please leave a chilled ice chest near your front door if you cannot be home to receive your delivery. If there is no cooler and no answer at your door, we will send you a notification via email and you may pick up your harvest at no charge from our distribution site during regular pickup hours. Thank you for your help and understanding!

We harvest only what you request. For this reason, we do not offer refunds for unclaimed produce. However, your food will be donated to our growers or a local food pantry.
